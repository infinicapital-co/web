About

Quiet, long-term, minimal

Infini Capital began with a simple goal: identify repeatable edges in liquid markets and execute them with discipline. The firm has evolved across Indian and U.S. markets, with work spanning equities, ETFs, bullion, futures, and event-driven opportunities.

The public-facing story stays intentionally understated. The real focus is process quality, risk control, and the ability to keep improving without noise.
