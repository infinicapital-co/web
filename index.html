About

Alpha lifecycle

Infini Capital began with a singular focus: using data to build strategies that work on intraday stock trading. Primarily operating in Indian equity markets across a universe of 4,000+ stocks, operations have since expanded to US equities, metals, bullion, IPOs, and futures.

Core operations evolved from manual discretionary trading to fully automated, system-driven trade execution — based on price action, volume, and risk management criteria. Every model is rigorously tested before live deployment.
